## Altitude Scroller

An interactive canvas experience that lets you scroll from ground level up through the atmosphere and into deep space. Features animated birds, hot air balloons, clouds, planes, satellites, UFOs, and a parallax star field — each appearing at their real-world altitude range.

## PDF Studio

A fully client-side PDF toolkit that runs entirely in the browser. Supports merging, splitting, rotating, and compressing PDFs — with thumbnail previews and drag-to-reorder. No files are ever uploaded; all processing happens locally using PDF.js and pdf-lib.
